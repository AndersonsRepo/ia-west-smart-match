# IA West Smart Match — Growth Strategy

## Executive Summary

IA West Smart Match transforms volunteer coordination from a manual, relationship-dependent process into a data-driven, scalable system. This growth strategy outlines how IA West can expand from the Cal Poly Pomona pilot to a regional platform serving 20+ universities across the West Coast.

## Current State (Pilot)

- **Coverage:** 1 university (Cal Poly Pomona)
- **Volunteers:** 17 IA West board members
- **Opportunities:** 15 CPP events + 35 course sections
- **Matching:** Manual, ad-hoc, dependent on personal connections

## Growth Phases

### Phase 1: CPP Deep Integration (Q2 2026)
**Goal:** Prove the model at Cal Poly Pomona with measurable outcomes.

- Deploy Smart Match for all CPP marketing department events and courses
- Achieve 10+ successful volunteer placements in first quarter
- Collect feedback from professors, event coordinators, and volunteers
- Refine matching algorithm based on real placement outcomes
- **KPI:** 80% coordinator satisfaction, 5+ guest lectures delivered

### Phase 2: Southern California Expansion (Q3 2026)
**Goal:** Expand to 3-5 additional SoCal universities.

- Target universities: UCLA, USC, SDSU, CSUF, Chapman
- Leverage existing board member connections in LA and San Diego metros
- Add web scraping module for automated opportunity discovery
- Integrate with university event calendars via public APIs
- **KPI:** 20+ active opportunities across 5 universities

### Phase 3: Full West Coast Coverage (Q4 2026 — Q1 2027)
**Goal:** Cover all IA West metro regions.

- Add Bay Area, Seattle, and Portland universities
- Assign metro directors as regional coordinators in the CRM
- Enable multi-chapter support for future national expansion
- Build reporting dashboard for IA West board
- **KPI:** 50+ active opportunities, 30+ successful placements

### Phase 4: National Platform (2027+)
**Goal:** Package Smart Match for other IA chapters.

- White-label the platform for IA East, IA Midwest, etc.
- Add industry partner matching (not just volunteers)
- Integrate with LinkedIn for professional profile enrichment
- Build API for university career centers to pull volunteer availability
- **KPI:** 3+ IA chapters using the platform

## User Acquisition Strategy

### For University Partners
1. **Dr. Yufan Lin as champion** — IA West board member AND CPP faculty; built-in credibility
2. **Faculty outreach** — Personalized emails showing relevant speaker matches for their courses
3. **Career center partnerships** — Position IA volunteers as industry engagement resources
4. **Student org sponsorship** — Offer IA speakers for club events (MISSA, SWIFT, etc.)

### For IA Volunteers
1. **Zero-friction onboarding** — Profile already populated from board roster
2. **Smart recommendations** — System finds and suggests opportunities (no browsing required)
3. **Automated scheduling** — Match recommendations align with IA event calendar
4. **Impact tracking** — Volunteers see their engagement history and membership conversions

## Competitive Advantage

| Feature | Manual Process | Smart Match |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Finding opportunities | Word-of-mouth | Automated discovery |
| Matching volunteers | Who's available? | Who's the best fit? |
| Outreach | Generic template | Personalized, data-driven |
| Tracking | Spreadsheet | Automated pipeline |
| Scaling | Linear (more people) | Exponential (more data) |

## Revenue Model (Future)

While IA West Smart Match starts as an internal tool, potential revenue paths include:
1. **SaaS for other professional associations** — $500-2,000/month per chapter
2. **University partnership fees** — Career centers pay for volunteer pipeline access
3. **Sponsored placements** — Companies sponsor volunteer slots for employer branding
4. **Data insights** — Anonymized trends on industry-academia engagement

## Risk Mitigation

- **Low adoption risk:** Start with warm introductions via Dr. Yufan Lin
- **Data quality risk:** Validate matching results with human review before scaling
- **Privacy risk:** All data is opt-in; responsible AI guidelines enforced
- **Sustainability risk:** Automated discovery reduces manual maintenance burden
